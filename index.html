Welcome to the Upside Down!!!

Stranger Things is a science fiction-horror streaming television series created by the Duffer brothers. Set in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana in the 1980s, the show follows a group of friends who uncover supernatural mysteries involving secret government experiments and parallel dimensions. The main characters, including Eleven, a young girl with psychokinetic abilities, and the Demogorgon, a monster from the Upside Down, have become iconic in popular culture. The show has received critical acclaim for its storytelling, characters, and 80s nostalgia, and has become one of Netflix's most successful original series, with four seasons currently released.
